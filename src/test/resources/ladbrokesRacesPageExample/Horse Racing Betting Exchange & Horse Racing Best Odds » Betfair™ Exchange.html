    Bet on Horse Racing, your way. Horse Racing on the Betfair Exchange lets you set the odds yourself on over 199 Horse Racing events. Here on the Exchange, you can back or lay bets on markets like "Gold Cup", "Champion Hurdle" and "Champion Chase", and get the odds you want.

    How to bet on Horse Racing

    Betting on Horse Racing is simple on the Betfair Exchange. Browse over 107 Horse Racing bets, choose a market, such as Antepost, Win, or SPECIAL, and then either match a bet, or set the odds and place a Horse Racing bet against others. You can bet for or against an outcome – e.g. if you're betting on Top GB NH Trainer 2017/18, you can place a lay bet if you think Paul Nicholls will lose, or you can place a back bet if you think Nicky Henderson will win.

    Understanding Horse Racing Betting Odds

    We show Horse Racing betting odds on the Exchange in decimals. Horse Racing decimal odds are easy to understand, because they represent the payout you'll get if you win – e.g. 7.7 means you'll receive 77 for every £10 you bet, including your stake, if the bet wins. The Horse Racing odds shown in the blue boxes are set by backers, and the odds shown in the pink boxes are set by the layers. As a Betfair member, you can ask for the Horse Racing odds that you want.
